Careers

We are Doing Great Work - Come Join Us!

Since its inception, Municipal Consultants has specialized in the municipal Water and Wastewater Industry. As a result, we have developed a highly qualified and experienced team of engineers, designers and support personnel. We are uniquely aware of the challenges faced by water and sewer systems; from regulatory compliance, to financial and rate analysis, to day to day operations.

Systems Engineer

Systems Engineer
Internal Services - Information Technology Services
Location – Birmingham, Alabama

  • Responsible for all aspects of systems availability, account provisioning and project provisioning.
  • Emphasis on operational excellence and continuous improvement of systems engineering-related processes and procedures.
  • Primary work will be work days local time, with some evenings, nights and weekends.
  • Takes part in on-call support rotation for production servers that require 24 X 7 X 365. Weekend or off hours work expected.
  • Plans, documents procedures and coordinate systems software/ hardware activities with local and remote team members and customers including team members and customers in India

Primary Qualifications Include:

  • A minimum of 4 years of relevant professional experience in a large, diverse, server environment in the following: Strong experience in a Microsoft Windows & Office desktop environment required to ensure a strong foundation in our customer’s environment and ability to document systems engineering efforts
  • Expert knowledge of VMware (required) and preferred knowledge of other virtualization technologies
  • Primary work will be work days local time, with some evenings, nights and weekends.
  • Deployment and operational expertise with Windows, Linux, and VMware
  • Demonstrated understanding of complex problem solving techniques dealing with systems engineering and support.

Administrative Assistant

Administrative Assistant
Reports to the CEO
Location – Birmingham, Alabama

  • Responsible for all incoming phone, fax & email inquiries. Greet guests and direct them to the appropriate contacts.
  • Manage meeting scheduling website and calendar; arrange food and beverage, and other details for coordinating office events.
  • Prepare, design and organize general office correspondences, memos, charts, tables, graphs, business plans, etc. Proofread copies for spelling, grammar and layout, and make appropriate changes. Prepare and edit presentations. Responsible for accuracy and clarity of final copy.
  • Coordinate office new hires, internships, vacation requests, expenses/budgets, benefits, and employee initiatives.
  • Plan and coordinate design labs, tradeshows and all related promotional events for the Eastern Region Sales Team.

Primary Qualifications Include:

  • Associates degree in Business Administration or related field or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Minimum of two (2) years of administrative experience
  • Clear and professional communication skills (verbal, written, presentation).
  • Able to work in a fast-paced environment with demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects and flexible to meet competing demands.
  • Able to work independently as well as collaboratively in a diverse group.
